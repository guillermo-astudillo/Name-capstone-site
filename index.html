What NAME does

A guided repair room for your public identity

NAME does not promise that every result can disappear. It helps you understand what is attached to your name, choose the right type of action, and keep a record of what you did next.

Cost, effort, and boundaries

What using NAME should feel like

Financial cost

The pilot is designed to be free or low-cost for users. Paid legal help may still be needed for complex cases.

Time cost

Most users should leave the first session with a map and one action plan, rather than a dozen open browser tabs.

Emotional cost

The interface uses calm language, stopping points, and limits so the process does not turn into panic-searching.

Legal limits

Privacy law is complex. NAME begins with Massachusetts users and explains when legal advice may be needed.
